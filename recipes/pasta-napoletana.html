Pasta Napoletana

Description

Ingredients

  • Pasta (bucatini, spaghetti, penneâ€¦)
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil or olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Finely dice the onion
  2. Add the diced onion and the oil to the pan.
  3. Saute the onion in olive oil until soft and translucent.
  4. Stir in the minced garlic until fragrant.
  5. Add canned tomatoes, salt and pepper.
  6. Cook the tomato sauce for 15 minutes.
  7. In the meantime cook and drain the pasta.
  8. Add pasta to the skillet along with fresh basil.
  9. Toss until well combined and juicy adding a touch of cooking water.
  10. Serve with freshly grated parmesan cheese and torn basil leaves.
